Project 1: Images of the Russian Empire
CS 180 - Fall 2024
By, Ediz Ertekin Jr.

For the project 1 of CS 180, our goal was to restore and align three color channel images from digitized glass plate images. We can do this by placing the three images on top of each other so that they form a single RGB color image. I first implemented a naive approach by exhasutive search to find the best alignment. Then, I implemented a more efficient approach for larger images such as .tif files by using a recursive method and an image pyramid.
